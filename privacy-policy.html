Privacy Policy

At Rezo, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights regarding your data. By using the Rezo application, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Such as your name, email address, and contact details provided during registration.
  • Reservation Details: Data about your clients and their reservations, including schedules, dates, and times.
  • Uploaded Content: Images and other files uploaded to the application.
  • Login Credentials: Securely hashed and stored passwords.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app to help us improve its functionality.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the core functionalities of the Rezo application.
  • Help you manage reservations and client information efficiently.
  • Allow you to upload and manage images related to your reservations.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of your login credentials and other data.
  • Analyze user interactions to enhance the user experience and develop new features.

How We Protect Your Information

We take data security seriously. The following measures are in place:

  • All passwords are securely hashed and stored, ensuring they cannot be easily decrypted.
  • Your uploaded images and files are encrypted and securely stored.
  • We use industry-standard security protocols to protect your data against unauthorized access.
  • Regular security audits and updates are conducted to maintain the integrity of our systems.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Data storage and backup to ensure your data is safe and always available.
  • Analytics to improve the performance and features of the application.
  • Security enhancements to protect your data from potential threats.
All third-party providers are compliant with international data protection standards.

Your Rights

As a user of Rezo, you have the following rights:

  • The right to access your personal data and reservation details stored in the application.
  • The right to modify or delete your information at any time.
  • The right to withdraw your consent for data usage where applicable.
For any inquiries or requests, please contact us at support@rezo.rs.
